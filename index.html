Shortcut Keys

You may use the following commands on your keyboard to access shortcuts for this player

  • ?
  • shortcut key listing - this menu
  • spacebar
  • play or pause the the music player
  • right arrow key
  • previous song
  • left arrow key
  • next song
  • up arrow key
  • increase volume
  • down arrow key
  • decrease volume
  • f
  • fast forwards music 10 seconds
  • d
  • rewind music 10 seconds
